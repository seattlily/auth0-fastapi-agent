Why this step?

Compass0 gates sensitive actions — admins creating or deleting Auth0 organizations, and travel agents booking or cancelling trips — on a fresh approval from your phone via Auth0's CIBA flow.

Enrolling once registers your device with Auth0 Guardian. Future approvals are pushed to your phone in real time, so you tap "Approve" and the action proceeds — no codes, no extra logins.
